Thank you for signing up at PeerJ, the Open Access publisher that Nature

We use email verification before activating your account to make sure you're 
human and that we have the right address. You may not be able to login until 
this is completed. If you didn't sign up to PeerJ then let us know at 
accounts@peerj.com.

Once you have activated your account with the link above, what next? Here 
are a few suggested next steps:

1.	Complete your profile details <https://peerj.com/settings/details/> , so 
that others know who you are.
2.	Claim your custom profile URL <https://peerj.com/settings/details/> 
before someone else gets it.
3.	Check out publishing plans for yourself <https://peerj.com/pricing/>  or 
let your institution know about the institutional plans 
<https://peerj.com/pricing/institutions/> .

And of course, please consider submitting your next manuscript to PeerJ (see 
author instructions for PeerJ <https://peerj.com/about/aims-and-scope/>  and 
PeerJ Computer Science <https://peerj.com/about/aims-and-scope/cs> ) or a 
free preprint <https://peerj.com/preprints/> .
